Zodiac Signs

What is Zodiac Sign?

The zodiac is a set of 12 constellations or groupings of stars that represent the Sun, Moon and Planets. Each constellation represents an animal or mythological character/creature that represents a period in life. Zodiac sign is a term used to describe the constellation that represents the sun's position on your date of birth. The twelve zodiac signs are Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn and Aquarius. Zodiac signs are the archetypes of the year, and each one represents a certain way of looking at the world. Each sign is associated with a particular element - Fire, Earth, Air, Water and has its own unique characteristics that make it stand out from the rest.
