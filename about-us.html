About Us

CelesExpedition is a free website that gives the user a bunch of information about our universe as we explore it. The purpose of this website is to share information that is already existing that may new to some and a new complete information that has been recently acquired or obtained by the scientists. The CelesExpedition website is a project that has been developed by two students from Technological Institute of the Philippines that took 1 month and 2 weeks, from October 27, 2023 to December 9,2023 to be fully developed.
